Local Areas:

Carlsbad

Carlsbad is a beutiful city on the coast of California, home to Bountiful Foods Headquarters, Legoland California, and Leo Carillo Ranch Historical Park. It features a prominent golf-manufaturing industry, earning it the nickname the "Titanium Valley".

Oceanside

Just North of Carlsbad, Oceanside is surrounded by exciting opportunities for family fun- Camp Pendleton, multiple golf courses, and of course the Ocean itself. You can't go wrong in Oceanside.

Encinitas

Looking for beaches? Encinitas has got them in spades, Swami Beach, Cardiff State Beach, Moonlight State Beach, and more! Not to mention the San-Diego Botanic Garden and the Batiquitos Lagoon State Marine Conservation Area and Bountiful Foods food trucks.

Solana Beach

Solana Beach is a historic coastal community, featuring the neighborhood of La Colonia de Eden Gardens, and the first city in continental US to enact a smoking ban on public beachs.

Santa Barbara

The setting of hit crime-comedy Psych, Santa Barbra is in fact a beutiful city and not the murder capital of California.
