Bountiful Foods website Planning Doc

Site Purpose

To provide information on Bountiful foods and allow people to order their products online.

Target Audience

The target audience is people trying to live a healthy lifestyle by eating fresh and organic foods but still want a degree of convenience. This includes both locals and visitors to the Carlsbad area, individuals, families, and smaller events.
