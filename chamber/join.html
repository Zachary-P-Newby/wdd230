Mebership Levels

Bronze

The Bronze level is our lowest membership level costing $15 a month, granting entry to the chamber rewards program, where businesses earn points each week which can be redeemed for prizes. It also includes access to social events like the Christmas dinner and Ice cream social.

Silver

The Silver level costs $30 a month, featuring all bronze level benefits and more. Including a 1.5x multiplier in the rewards program, free chamber stationary, access to the febuary pancake breakfast, and access to our monthly continuing education seminar.

Gold

Costing $100 a month, the gold membership, features access to all availible benfits. Including all social events, double rewards points, advanced training seminars, free office supplies each month from Ruby Co. warhous, access to the chamber sauna, and timeshare on West Side Island.

Non-Profit

Exclusive to Non-Profit organizations, the Non-profit level has no membership fees. It features access to our special Non-profit biannual dinner, up to $100 of supplies free from the Ruby Co. warehouse, and bonus points in our Chamber rewards program. The Non-profit level also includes all Bronze level benefits.
