Carl Junction Chamber of Commerce Siteplan

Homepage Target Audience Design

Target Audience

The primary target audience of the CJCC website is current or prospective businesses and inhabitants of Carl Junction and the surrounding area looking for information on Carl Junction businesses and events.

Audience Personas

Local Contractor

Name

Ed McGooberson

Demographics

  • Age: 35
  • Occupation: Construction Contractor
  • Tech Proficency: Average
  • Personality: Hardworking, good-natured, and stubborn.
  • Likes: Efficiency, simplicity, Croissants, and easy-to-navigate websites.
  • Dislikes: Confusing layouts, hard-to-find information, and cats

Bio

Ed is a no-nonsense, hard-working fellow, he manages his company honestly and treats his employees well, but has little patience for misbehavior. He believes in working with others even though he sometimes disagrees and finds them annoying, so he tries to keep in touch with other businesses.

Behavior

If Ed can find what information he is looking for he will head straight to it, get what he needs, and leave. Otherwise, he will search for up to 10 minutes, get frustrated, and leave.

Objective

Ed is looking for networking and employee training opportunities. He has come to the website for information on the next mixer, as the contact information for the new pie bakery on Main Street.

Hard working Secretary

Name

Heather Dithers

Demographics

  • Age: 25
  • Occupation: Secretary at RedLeaf Furniture Company
  • Tech Proficiency: More than average, skilled with Excel, but no programming experience
  • Personality: Cheery, outgoing, studious, but easily distracted.
  • Likes: Good-looking UI, short but informative pieces of information, animals, and beekeeping.
  • Dislikes: Long chunks of text she has to simplify, unresponsive websites, and people who hurt animals

Bio

Heather is the secretary of the regional manager of the Joplin RedLeaf Furniture division. She is outgoing and works hard to provide for her boss's and co-workers' needs. However she is easily distracted and often has to explain things to her busy boss in short periods, so she dislikes long chunks of info she has to break down and simplify. She is thinking of taking up beekeeping in her spare time.

Behavior

Heather has to work quickly to meet her boss's and co-workers' needs, so she tries to get things done quickly and efficiently. She often has to break down information into its most simple and concise form, so she will avoid large blocks of text. She is patient and will search for hard-to-find information for up to 20 minutes, but she usually switches websites or refines her search after five minutes.

Objective

Heather needs to keep track of local events and other businesses for her boss, as well as gather information quickly, so she regularly reviews the bulletin board and calendar on the CJCC website and posts the job listings for her company.

Scenarios

  • Jill Mitchell, a 45-year-old local mother of 4, has come to the CJCC website in search of potential job opportunities for her oldest child that is in town. Her needs will be met primarily by the job listings and calendar pages.
  • J. A. Cob is a 27-year-old man who intends to move to Carl Junction and open a new RubyCo. outlet there. He has come to the website for information on the business landscape and intends to join the CJCC. He will view the About page, as well as the pages of various local businesses, before contacting a CJCC member about joining.
  • Earl May is a 32-year-old tech support specialist who wishes to switch to a new employer with better wages and benefits. He does not want to move out of the Joplin area, so he viewing the job openings and various businesses he could offer his services to. He will view the job listings, browse various businesses in the CJCC, and check the calendar for mixers and other opportunities.
  • Curtis Smewby is a 52-year-old optometrist and the owner of Glaicer VisionCare. He is a member of the CJCC and has come on the website to look for an exterminator to spray his office for bugs. He searches for the contact information of an exterminator in the chamber and gives him a call about the job.
  • Jimmy Django is the 42-year-old owner of a local catering service. He has a job opening for a new chef, so he has logged onto the CJCC website to post it. He will sign in and post the opening on the listings page, and wait for someone to answer.
