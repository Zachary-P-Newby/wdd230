Carl Junction Chamber of Commerce Siteplan

Homepage Target Audience Design

Target Audience

The primary target audience of the CJCC website are current or prospective businesses and inhabitants of Carl Junction and the surrounding area looking for information on Carl Junction businesses and events.

Audience Personas

Local Contractor

Name

Ed McGooberson

Demographics

  • Age: 35
  • Occupation: Construction Contractor
  • Tech Proficency: Average
  • Personality: Hard working, good-natured, and stubborn.
  • Likes: Efficiency, simplicity, Corissants, and easy to navigate websites.
  • Dislikes: Consfusing layouts, hard to find information, and cats

Bio

Ed is a no-nosense hard working fellow, he manages company honestly, and treats his employees well, but has little patience for misbehavior. He believes in working with others even though he sometimes disagrees and finds them annoying, so he tries keep in touch with other businesses.

Behavior

If Ed can find what information he is looking for he will head straight to it, get what he needs, and leave. Otherwise he will search up to 10 minutes, get frustrated, and leave.

Objective

Ed is looking for newtworking and employee training opportunities. He has come to the website for information on the next mixer, as the contact information for the new pie bakery on mainstreet.

Hard working Secretary

Name

Heather Dithers

Demographics

  • Age: 25
  • Occupation: Secretary at RedLeaf Furniture Company
  • Tech Proficency: More than average, skilled with excel, but no programming expirience
  • Personality: Cheery, outgoing, studious, but easily distracted.
  • Likes: Good looking UI, short but informative pieces of information, animals and beekeeping.
  • Dislikes: Long chunks of text she has to simplify, unresponsive websites, and people who hurt animals

Bio

Heather is the secratary of the regional manger of the Joplin RedLeaf Furniture division. She is outgoing and works hard to provide for her boss and co-worker's needs. However she is easily distracted and often has to explain things in to her busy boss in short periods of time, so she dislikes long chunks of info she has to break down and simplify. She is thinking of taking up beekeeping in her spare time.

Behavior

Heather has to work quickly to meet her boss and co-workers' needs, so she tries to get things done quickly and efficently. She often has to breakdown information into its most simple and concise form, so she will avoid large blocks of text. She is patient and will search for hard to find information up to 20 minutes, but she usually switches websites or refines her serach affter five minutes.

Objective

Heather needs to keep track of local events and other businessse for her boss, as well gather information quickly, so she regular reviews the bulletin board and calendar on the CJCC website and posts the job listings for her company.

Scenarios

  • Jill Mitchell, a 45 year old local mother of 4, has come to the CJCC website in search of potential job opportunities for her oldest child that are in town. Her needs will be met primarily by in the job listings and calendar pages.
  • J. A. Cob is a 27 year old man who intends to move to Carl Junction and open a new outlet of the RubyCo. store chain there. He has come to website for information on the business landscape and intend to join the CJCC. He will view the about page, as well as the pages of various local businesses, before contacting a CJCC member about joining.
  • Earl May is an 32 year old tech support specialist he wishes to switch to a new employer with better wages and benefits. He does not want to move out of the Joplin area, so he viewing the job openings and various businesses he could offer his services to. He will view the job listings, browse various businesses in the CJCC, and check the calendar for mixers and other opportunities.
  • Curtis Smewby is a 52 year old optomotrist and the owner of Glaicer VisionCare. He is meber of the CJCC, and has come on the website to look for an exterminator to spray his office for bugs. He search up the contact information of an exterminator in the the chamber and give him a call about the job.
  • Jimmy Django is the 42 year old owner of a local catering service. He has a job opening for a new chef, so he has logged onto the CJCC website to post it. He will sign in and post the opening on the listings page, and wait for someone to answer.
