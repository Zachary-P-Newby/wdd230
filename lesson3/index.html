Site Purpose

The purpose of the Carl Junction Chamber of Commerce (or CJCC)'s' website is to provide an easy place for people to find information on Carl Junction's businesses, events, and the Chamber itself. This includes an online bulletin board, calendar, about page, job listings, and pages providing information on local businesses. The CJCC aims to serve the local businesses and the community and so does its website. Information that could be found on the website includes business directions, community events, contacts, and job openings.
